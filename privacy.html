Manga Drama Studio

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Manga Drama Studio is a creator production and publishing operations tool. This policy explains what information the service is designed to process when used with official platform APIs.

Information processed

The system may process the following information for production and publishing operations:

  • Authorized story text, episode plans, shot plans, subtitles, captions, and generated media metadata.
  • Creator account handles, publishing schedules, post identifiers, share URLs, and performance metrics.
  • OAuth authorization results such as granted scopes, access token expiry, refresh token expiry, and open IDs.

Information not requested

Manga Drama Studio does not require account passwords, browser cookies, personal banking information, or private messages. Operators should never enter such information into the system.

OAuth tokens

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are read from secure runtime storage or environment variables. Token values must not be committed to GitHub, synced folders, logs, demo outputs, or public pages.

Use of information

Information is used to prepare episodes, validate publishing readiness, call authorized APIs, and monitor posts.

Sharing

Manga Drama Studio is not designed to sell personal information. Information may be sent to API providers only when necessary for the configured production or publishing workflow and only with the relevant operator's authorization.

Retention

Operators control project files and local databases. Generated outputs and metrics should be deleted when they are no longer needed for production, compliance, or business records.

Contact

Support and privacy requests are handled by the app operator listed in the TikTok Developer application for Manga Drama Studio. Users may also contact the operator through the support contact supplied during app review.

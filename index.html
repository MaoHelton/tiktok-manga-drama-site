Manga Drama Studio

A production and operations tool for creators who turn authorized fiction text into short vertical animated episodes, review quality, and publish approved videos through official platform APIs.

What it does

  • Converts licensed novel text into episode plans, shots, captions, subtitles, and render tasks.
  • Locks character and style references to reduce drift across long-running daily series.
  • Runs media QA and manual review before any video enters a publishing queue.
  • Uses official OAuth and posting APIs. It does not ask for account passwords or browser cookies.

Licensed input

The workflow is designed for authorized source material. Operators must confirm adaptation rights before producing or publishing any episode.

AI production

The system separates story adaptation, image keyframes, video shots, voice, subtitles, assembly, and QA so output quality can be tested and improved step by step.

Publishing controls

Publishing plans are generated only after quality and rights gates pass. Access tokens are read from secure runtime storage and are never committed to source control.
